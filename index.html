11th West Coast Workshop in International Finance

April 10, 2026 · Santa Clara University · The de Saisset Museum

Agenda 2026

Friday, April 10, 2026 — The de Saisset Museum, Santa Clara University.

  • 8:30–9:00 Coffee and Rolls

Geopolitics, Fragmentation, and Global Capital Allocation

Chair: Chris Meissner (UC Davis)

  • 9:00–9:45 “U.S. Military Protection, Allies’ Portfolios, and Exorbitant Privilege,” Paolo Cavallino (BIS), Daniel Jimenez (Louvain), Alessandro Rebucci (JHU).

    Discussant: Colin Weiss (Fed Board)

  • 9:45–10:30 “Breaking Up: Fragmentation in Foreign Direct Investment,” Cody Kallen (Fed Board).

    Discussant: Grace Gu (UCSC)

  • 10:30–10:45 Break

Incomplete Markets, Mobility, and Wealth

Chair: Galina Hale (UC Santa Cruz)

  • 10:45–11:30 “Misallocation due to Incomplete Markets,” David Baqaee (UCLA), Ariel Burstein (UCLA).

    Discussant: Minjie Deng (SFU)

  • 11:30–12:15 “The Geography of Wealth: Shocks, Mobility, and Precautionary Savings,” Maximiliano Dvorkin (St. Louis Fed), Brian Greaney (UW).

    Discussant: Jordan Rosenthal-Kay (FRBSF)

  • 12:15–1:45 Lunch

Globalization and Cross-Border Flows

Chair: Paul Bergin (UC Davis)

  • 1:45–2:30 “Globalization Accounting,” Sterre Kuipers (UH), Hyunju Lee (UH), Radek Paluszynski (UH). Discussant: Chenyue Hu (UCSC)
  • 2:30–3:15 “Multinationals and Uncertainty: The Role of Internal Capital Markets,” Jianlin Wang (SCU). Discussant: Zheng Liu (FRBSF)
  • 3:15–3:30 Break

Global Credit Conditions and Monetary Transmission

Chair: Wentong Chen (SCU)

  • 3:30–4:15 “The Global Credit Cycle,” Nina Boyarchenko (FRBNY), Leonardo Elias (FRBNY). Discussant: Saroj Bhattarai (UT Austin)
  • 4:15–5:00 “A Model of U.S. Monetary Policy and the Global Financial Cycle,” Rohan Kekre (UC Berkeley Haas), Moritz Lenel (Princeton). Discussant: Emile Marin (UCD)
  • 5:00–7:30 Reception and Dinner

The workshop is sponsored by the Federal Reserve Bank of San Francisco and the Department of Economics at Santa Clara University.

Organizing committee: Vito Cormun (co-chair, SCU), Helen Popper (co-chair, SCU), Sanjay Singh (co-chair, FRBSF & UCD), Paul Bergin (UCS), Antonio Coppola (Stanford GSB), Galina Hale (UCSC), Viktoria Hnatkovska (UBC), Ina Simonovska (UCD), Mark Spiegel (FRBSF), Chenzi Xu (UCB).
